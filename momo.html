Vegetable Momos Recipe

About Vegetable Momos Recipe | Momos Recipe:

These popular street-style momos stand as the most favourite food for kids today. Momos, originating from Tibet is a popular street food in the country, largely in North-India. A versatile food that it is, we have a wide variety available from chicken, mutton to vegetable momos. Since the kids love the dish so much, it is always great to make it at home. You just need to master the technique and you can twist them to suit your palate.

Ingredients of Vegetable Momos Recipe | Easy Tibetan Momos Recipe:

These dumplings are packed with healthy veggies like carrots and cabbage sauteed with onion, garlic, soya sauce, vinegar and black pepper. The perfect homemade vegetarian snack to serve for evening snacks.

Total Cook Time50 mins

Prep Time10 mins

Cook Time40 mins

Recipe Servings10

Ingredients of Vegetable Momos


  • For dough:

  • 2 cups Maida

  • 1/2 tsp Salt

  • 1/2 tsp Baking powder

  • For filling:

  • 1 cup Carrot, grated

  • 1 cup Cabbage, grated

  • 1 tbsp Oil

  • 1/2 cup Onion, finely chopped

  • 1 tsp Garlic, chopped

  • 1 tsp Soya sauce

  • to taste Salt

  • 1/4 tsp Vinegar

  • 1/4 tsp Black pepper

How to Make Vegetable Momos


  1. Mix the maida, salt and baking powder and knead to a stiff dough with water.

  2. Heat oil and add the onion and garlic. Saute over high heat and add the carrot and cabbage. Turn around over high heat till glossy.

  3. Take it off the heat and mix in the soya sauce, salt, vinegar and black pepper.

  4. Roll the dough thin (translucent) and cut into 4"-5" rounds.

  5. Take a round, wet edges and place some filling in the center.

  6. Bring edges together to cover the filling. Twist to seal and fill the rest in the same way.

  7. Steam for about 10 minutes and serve with soya sauce and chilli sauce.
